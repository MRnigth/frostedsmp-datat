How To Tap
A KeyKeg

Set-up guide  ·  20L KeyKeg  ·  Air / CO₂
1

Chill it first

Keep the keg cold before tapping. Store cold, serve cold — a warm keg pours all foam.

2

Stand it up & uncap

Set the KeyKeg upright on a stable surface. Pull off the cap and check the spear valve is clean.

3

Fit the coupler

Push the KeyKeg coupler straight down onto the valve and lock the lever until it clicks tight.

4

Connect gas & beer line

Attach the gas / air line (drives the bag) and the beverage line to the dispense outlet.

5

Set the pressure

Open the gas and set the regulator to the serving pressure (approx. 2–2.5 bar). Do not over-pressurise.

6

Pour & enjoy

Open the tap and pour. When empty: close gas, vent, disconnect, then recycle the KeyKeg.

!

KeyKeg is pressurised. Never exceed the rated working pressure, keep the keg upright while connected, and release gas before disconnecting.
